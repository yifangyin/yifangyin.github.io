Yifang Yin

PhD, National University of Singapore, 2016

Senior Research Fellow
Institute of Data Science
National University of Singapore
Email:
Lab: Grab-NUS AI Lab

Expertise: Machine Learning, Multimedia Analysis & Spatiotemporal Data Mining »

I am a Senior Research Fellow in Grab-NUS AI Lab at National University of Singapore supervised by Prof. Roger Zimmermann and Prof. See-Kiong Ng. I was a Research Intern at the Incubation Center, Research and Technology Group, Fuji Xerox Co., Ltd., Japan, from October, 2014 to March, 2015. I received the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from National University of Singapore, Singapore, in 2016, and received the B.E. degree in Department of Computer Science and Technology from Northeastern University, Shenyang, China, in 2011.

My research interests include machine learning, multimodal multimedia analysis, and spatiotemporal data mining.
